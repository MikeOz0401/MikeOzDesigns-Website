Who Am I?

Mike Ozarek | MEng GMICE

Hi, I am Mike! I am a passionate architectural designer who has always dreamed of shaping every aspect of an architectural project, such as designing interiors, exteriors or gardens. Nothing makes me happier than sitting down with people, discussing their ideas, and translating them into spaces they can truly enjoy living in. Helping people turn their visions into reality is truly why I love to do this.

Academically, I hold a MEng degree in Civil Engineering with Architecture from the University of Glasgow; from which I graduated in 2024. However, my journey started in Glasgow Kelvin College, where I initially developed a passion for tackling & solving tough structural engineering problems. During my time here I was able to excel academically, achieving top grades in my class, which was quickly followed by my transition to university to continue pursuing a career in the Civil sector of Engineering. However, the transition was not simple, as I knew that despite having a passion for solving structural problems ... I also really enjoyed tackling the design aspect of those same projects; often finding myself doodling fa√ßade designs, structural connection details, garden layouts, residential houses, flats, modern villas and more.

It was at that point I knew that pursuing just one aspect of the industry would not be enough for me. Luckily for me, I did not have to choose, as I was offered a place at the University of Glasgow which offered a course in both Civil Engineering, as well as Architecture, which was exclusively taught by licensed Architects at the Glasgow School of Art. It was easy to say that this course proved to be perfect for me. Here, I was able to fully develop my understanding of the complex theories surrounding structural engineering, as well as my skills and abilities at crafting architectural designs from the idea conception stage, all the way to the final renditions. This unique combination of disciplines empowers me to approach designs from a unique angle, as I am not only able to bring your most complex visions and ideas to life, but I am also able to ensure they are practical and realistic.

Professionally, I balance my roles as a Graduate Structural Engineer at Jacobs, with my position as a Lead Designer at MikeOzDesigns. Through these roles I can continue developing my structural engineering knowledge; by working towards my CEng as a structural engineer, in addition to further developing my creative skills by pursuing project on the side when given the opportunity. Commitment to innovation and excellence drives me to continuously seek opportunities that blend my technical expertise with my creative aspirations, enhancing the built environment in meaningful ways.

Services

What Can I Do For You?

Let's work together to bring your vision to life!
Discover the range of services I provide below.

Conceptual & Developed Design | Planning Permission

Looking to make some changes around your home? Are you considering adding an extension, converting a loft into a room, transforming your old spare room into a bathroom or maybe re-arranging your garden layout?

I can help design, visualise, draft and submit your plans to your local council for approval. As part of my standard service, I include visualisations for all my projects during this process. This means that throughout the design phase, I'll create 3D visualisations of the designed spaces or proposed changes, helping you see the results before they're a reality.

Additionally, I'll also keep you informed of the entire process in detail, ensuring everything is clear and that you feel comfortable throughout.

Technical Design | Building Warrant

Alongside a Planning application, a Building Warrant will most likely also be required to obtain permission from your local council; which is a legal requirement prior to beginning any construction project.

While I'm currently unable to sign off on SER certificates, my experience in structural engineering enables me to draft designs that take into account the structural side of things. This means that my solutions will most likely not change majorly after a review from a structural engineer.

Additionally, to make the process less stressful for you, I can contact and work with other structural engineers to draft a proper building warrant application on your behalf, ensuring the application process goes smoothly.

Permitted Development | No Building Warrant

Not looking for any big changes? Are you just planning on building a garage or swimming pool in your garden, transforming your living room into an open plan living space, redesigning your bedroom to an office space or any other small work that you can complete yourself or with your local joiner?

Your proposal may fall under Permitted Development, which does not require a Building Warrant or Planning Permission from your local council, if designed to the rules and regulations.

Should this be the case, I can help draft, design and bring your idea to reality, helping you save money along the way & enable you to start the construction process much faster than you would usually be able to.

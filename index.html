About

Who am I?

Mike Ozarek | MEng GMICE

I hold a Master’s degree in Civil Engineering with Architecture from the University of Glasgow, where I developed a deep passion for both structural engineering and creative design. Starting my academic journey at Glasgow Kelvin College at just 17, I excelled in my studies, achieving top grades and laying a strong foundation for my dual-focused career. This unique combination of disciplines empowers me to approach projects with both creative vision and practical precision, ensuring solutions that are not only innovative but also highly functional.

Professionally, I balance my roles as a Graduate Structural Engineer at Jacobs with my position as Lead Designer of MikeOzDesigns. This dual engagement allows me to integrate robust engineering practices with aesthetic design principles, enabling me to deliver holistic and high-quality outcomes whether I'm engineering a bridge or redesigning a home. My commitment to innovation and excellence drives me to continuously seek opportunities that blend my technical expertise with my creative aspirations, ultimately enhancing the built environment in meaningful ways.

Burrell in the City

As the ultimate design project in the MEng Programme, this exercise offers you the opportunity to demonstrate a synthesis of your applied knowledge of architecture and engineering gained over your period of study. The aim of the project is to demonstrate a comprehensive design proposal that meets both the structural and spatial needs of the project brief. The project explores the structural and spatial challenges of designing a multi-storey building within an urban context, with a diverse range of spaces, both in terms of programmatic requirements and dimension, that could become new home spaces for multiple pieces from the burrell collection that are outlined in a later part of this portfolio.

Services

What I can do for you

Let's work together to bring your vision to life!
Discover the range of services I provide below.

Conceptual & Developed Design | Planning Permission

Looking to make some changes around your home? Whether you're considering an extension, a loft conversion, room redesigns, or landscape designs, I can help design, visualise, draft, and submit your plans to your local council for approval. As part of my standard service, I include visualizations for all my clients' projects during the planning permission process. Throughout the design phase, I'll create 3D visualizations of the redesigned spaces or proposed changes, helping you see the space before it's built. I'll also explain the entire process in detail and keep you informed every step of the way, ensuring you understand and feel comfortable throughout.

Technical Design | Building Warrant

While I'm currently unable to sign off on structural drawings, my experience in structural engineering enables me to draft detailed plans that can be reviewed by a licensed structural engineer. I can collaborate with other structural engineers to help create an application for a building warrant, ensuring the process moves smoothly. To make the process easier on my clients, I will contact structural engineers to ease the stress for you during this process. FILLerFILLerFILLerFILLer

Permitted Development | No Building Warrant Designs

Not looking for any big changes? If you just want to redesign your bathroom, bedroom, or living room with your local joiner or on your own, I can help. I'll measure your specific space, draft ideas, and present them through drawings and visualizations. This will aid whoever is undertaking the work to transform your home, reducing the risk of anything going wrong. Additionally, I can offer guidance on what to avoid and the best courses of action to circumvent the need for planning permission or a building warrant.
